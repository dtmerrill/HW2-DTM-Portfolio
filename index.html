The Mind of Dana Merrill...

A Frankensteinian approach to development and project/program management.

An artist using the best tools and materials can make magic. Often, however, only faulty tools are at hand and all the materials must be scrounged. Original thoughts of 'perfection' fly away and the need for something that works becomes the only goal. Dana has found himself in the lab many times after visits to the graveyard driving toward life in his code, project and program experiments and found it. Thankfully, not once has his creation destroyed him.
